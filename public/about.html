About The Yashio Kenya

Our vision is "Realizing a world where people can create their own future".

We are going to build schools all over the world to realize it.

We believe that the essence of education is to provide the “Survival Intelligence” necessary for society.

We provide a service called "Skilled". We provide a range of services from the development of educational content ,systems , to post-learning job support for persons.

We welcome anyone who can help us achieve this goal.
